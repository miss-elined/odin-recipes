Buchthorn Tea

Description

Delicious drink!

Ingredients

  • Buchthorn berries
  • Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put together berries and a quater of water in your saucepan. Turn on the heat.
  2. When the berries become soft enough, squash them with a glass.
  3. Add the rest of the water and wait for tea to boil.
  4. Add sugar.
